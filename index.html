BOM District Showdown - Rules & Notes
Basic Rules
No drama - be respectful to everyone
Do NOT change your in-game name during the event so we can track gold & purchases accurately
Be online to roll dice on Friday and weekends for best possible ranking
How It Works
New to DS? Watch the overview below - at 1:10 it shows the exact pack you need to purchase
Your Commitment
Purchase 1x merchant pack (the pack that gives 1 dice to everyone) = 1x 1999 gold
All packs must be purchased before Saturday 16:00 UTC
If you need to leave the alliance before the event ends, make sure your dice are at 0 - they reset on leave
Don't leave after Saturday 16:00 UTC - 24h in alliance is required for gold distribution
Keep your key to raid a specific alliance (announced in next mail, Sunday 12:00 UTC)
Our Commitment
90% of gold donations refunded at event end - only if you complete your commitment
Distribute Merit Chests to top DS rankers
Open the gate for your farm accounts (ask in alliance chat)
Send reminders throughout the week - Horde schedule, alliance to raid in DS, etc.
Sponsoring Rules
You must complete your purchase pack for sponsoring someone, please let us know who you are sponsoring.
Sponsors must buy extra pack to cover the player they sponsoring - e.g. sponsoring 2 people = buying 3 packs total
Sponsored players show Sponsored status; the sponsor's pack count reflects their total purchases
Sponsorship notes are visible in the Note column of the list

Questions? Ask R4 or especially: Sarci · BoMNOX · Amanda · MissCake

Have fun, have a laugh, and thank you for joining
